Sinigang Recipe

  • Knorr's Asim-kilig Mix
  • Pork
  • Vinegar
  • Magic Sarap
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking steps:

  1. Dice onions, garlic, and carrots.
  2. On a hot pan with cooking oil then, sautee onions, and garlic.
  3. Sear pork with sauteed onions and garlic, until brown.
  4. Add water and sinigang mix.
  5. Simmer for 30 to 45 minutes until pork is tender.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
