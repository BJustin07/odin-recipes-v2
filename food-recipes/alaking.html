Alaking Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Evaporada Milk
  • Butter
  • Corn
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking steps:

  1. Dice onions, garlic, and carrots.
  2. On a hot pan drop 3tbsp of butter then, sautee carrots,onions,garlic and corn.
  3. Add flour and slowly mix-in the Evaporada Milk. Continuously stirring
  4. Add pepper and salt to taste
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
