Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 pound chicken thighs or pork belly, cut into pieces
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Cooking steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, water, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Mix well.
  2. Marinate the chicken or pork in the mixture for at least 30 minutes, preferably overnight.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Remove the meat from the marinade and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Pour the marinade into the pan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the meat is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
